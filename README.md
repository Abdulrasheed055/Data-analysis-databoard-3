#  Project Overview

Toman Bike Shop wanted to better understand its sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and pricing strategy to support data-driven business decisions.

This project combines **SQL** for data cleaning and analysis with **Power BI** for interactive visualization, enabling stakeholders to monitor business performance, evaluate customer segments, identify peak sales periods, and assess pricing opportunities.

The dashboard provides executives with a centralized view of key performance indicators (KPIs) to improve profitability and optimize operational planning.

---

#  Business Problem

As sales volume continued to grow, management required answers to several strategic questions:

- Which customer segment generates the most revenue?
- Is the business operating profitably?
- Which products contribute the most to revenue?
- When do peak demand periods occur?
- Can product prices be increased without significantly reducing demand?
- How can customer satisfaction be maintained during busy periods?

Without a centralized reporting solution, identifying these trends was time-consuming and limited strategic decision-making.

#  Key Business Insights

##  Strong Financial Performance

The business generated approximately **$10 million in total revenue**, producing **$7.03 million in profit** with an overall **45% profit margin**.

### Business Insight

A 45% profit margin indicates strong operational efficiency and healthy pricing, providing room for strategic investment and future business growth.

---

##  Customer Segmentation

Registered riders account for **81.81%** of all customers, while casual riders represent only **18.19%**.

### Business Insight

The business benefits from a loyal customer base. Expanding membership incentives could further strengthen retention and increase recurring revenue.

---

##  Peak Sales Periods

Sales activity is highest during **midday and evening hours**, particularly on **Fridays and Saturdays**.

### Business Insight

Peak demand periods require optimized staffing, inventory management, and customer support to maintain service quality and maximize revenue opportunities.

---

##  Product Performance

Analysis identified the highest-performing products as the primary contributors to total sales and profitability.

### Business Insight

Focusing inventory planning and promotional campaigns on best-selling products can further improve revenue growth while minimizing stock shortages.

---

##  Pricing Analysis

Scenario analysis indicates that a **10–15% price increase** may improve profitability without significantly affecting customer demand.

### Business Insight

A gradual pricing adjustment allows the business to increase revenue while minimizing the risk of customer churn.

---

##  Customer Purchasing Behavior

Registered riders purchase more consistently than casual riders and contribute a larger share of overall revenue.

### Business Insight

Targeted promotions encouraging casual riders to become registered members can improve customer lifetime value and revenue stability.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Implement a Gradual Price Increase

Introduce a controlled **10–15% price adjustment** while closely monitoring customer demand and sales performance.

---

### 2. Strengthen Customer Loyalty Programs

Expand rewards, discounts, and membership benefits to encourage customer retention and increase repeat purchases.

---

### 3. Optimize Peak-Time Operations

Increase staffing and inventory availability during high-demand periods, particularly on Fridays, Saturdays, and peak business hours.

---

### 4. Focus Marketing on High-Performing Products

Allocate marketing resources toward products generating the highest revenue while evaluating underperforming products for optimization.

---

### 5. Convert Casual Riders into Members

Develop targeted campaigns encouraging casual customers to register through exclusive discounts and loyalty incentives.

---

### 6. Continuously Monitor Pricing Performance

Track sales trends, customer feedback, and profitability following pricing adjustments to ensure long-term revenue growth.
